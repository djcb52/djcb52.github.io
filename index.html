Ripple – Your Secure Offline Vault

No Cloud. No Ads. No Tracking.

Ripple is a privacy-first data manager for Android and Windows. Store passwords, notes, files, and records locally—fully under your control.

Manual backups, strong encryption, and an offline-first design make Ripple a simple but powerful tool for people who want digital independence.
